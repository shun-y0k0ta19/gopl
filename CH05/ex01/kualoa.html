Journey through the beauty and tranquility of this special place while on our Hawaiian eco-tours and activities through many of the sites made famous by the many Hollywood movies and television shows filmed on property.

The Kualoa Private Nature Reserve is legendary to ancient Hawaiians and to the Hollywood film industry, and is also a working cattle ranch and farm, preserved and managed by the sixth generation kama’aina family (the Morgan ‘ohana) who consider themselves stewards of the land. Your visit to the Kualoa Private Nature Reserve contributes to the Kualoa Private Nature Reserve ‘ohana’s (family) efforts to preserving the natural beauty and cultural significance for future generations of locals and visitors to enjoy. For more, see Kualoa History.
